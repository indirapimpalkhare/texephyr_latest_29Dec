REVERSE ENGINEERING

Details

Max. No. Of Participants: 4

Registration Fees: Rs 250/- per team

No. of Rounds 2

INTRODUCTION:

In this competition the participant has to prove his understanding of fundamental concepts as an engineer. It requires them to have a systematic approach to analysing and reasoning the engineering design of devices and systems.

EVENT RULES

  • Round 1:
    • An online quiz based on aptitude, automobile and basic mechanical knowledge.
  • Round 2:
    • The teams will be given a mechanical device (Declared on the spot) which they are required to disassemble to the smallest components, note down the Specifications of each component.
    • The teams qualifying will be selected on the basis of time taken during the process and the accuracy of the job.
  • Round 3:
    • The teams will then divide themselves in 2 pairs. One pair will perform a CAD model and the other will reassemble the device.
    • CAD design for the assembly should be done using the specifications gathered earlier.
    • You cannot reassemble the device unless there is a proper CAD model ready for the device.
    • Assessment of this round will be based on the time taken and accuracy of both the CAD model as well as the physical assembly.

Problem statement will be displayed 20 days prior the competition on the event website. (Link - www.texephyr.com)

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

Any student with valid ID card of their educational institute can participate
